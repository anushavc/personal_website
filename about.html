About

"Building systems that don't just solve today's problems, but evolve how we approach them entirely"

I am a data scientist currently pursuing my Master's in Information Management at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign, building upon my foundation in Computer Science and Engineering from Vellore Institute of Technology.

I believe that every line of code should serve a purpose beyond just functionality. While building AI systems and data pipelines, I like challenging conventional approaches, asking not just "Does it work?" but "Is this the best way to solve this problem?". This mindset has driven me to architect solutions that don't merely meet requirements but fundamentally transform how businesses operate – whether through smarter document retrieval systems, optimized data processing, or innovative machine learning implementations.

My approach is rooted in the belief that technology should work smarter, not harder – but, more importantly, it should create a meaningful impact. When I tackle a challenge, I dive deep into understanding both the technical landscape and the human element it serves.
